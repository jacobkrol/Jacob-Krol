Simulating Magnetic Waves

Advisor: Mimi Dai

Sponsoring Organization: Math Computing Lab, UIC

August 2019 - December 2019

  • This project was oriented towards visualizations of magnetic waves through Navier-Stokes differential equation solvers. This allows for the subsequent development of fluid simulators and observational physics tools.
  • Static and dynamic simulations have been built in Python 3 and Sage Math, using packages such as matplotlib and numpy, and in JavaScript. Their development has required solvers for partial and ordinary differential equation systems.

Efficiency of Planar Disk Packings

Advisor: Ali Mohajer

Sponsoring Organization: Math Computing Lab, UIC

January 2018 - January 2019

  • This research pursues identifying the ideal ratios of both disk size and disk amounts in an infinite plane that provide an optimization of packing density.
  • Important concepts have included Voronoi decompositions and geometric relationships in programming, using Python, JavaScript, and C++

Wikipedia Illustration Task Force

Advisor: Jan Verschelde

Sponsoring Organization: Math Computing Lab, UIC

  • Important concepts included Kempe’s Universality Theorem, parameterization of trigonometric functions, serial chain composition, and mechanical gearing ratios, using Sage Math.
